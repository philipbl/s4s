Venue Information

The conference will be held at in the event space at BYU's Engineering Building, which is located at the south east end of campus (here). There are a few options for getting to the Engineering Building.

Car

There are a few lots around campus that guests can park at. The picture below shows the lots along with directions on how to get to the front of the building.

Public Transportation

The Engineering is a two minute walk from the UVX BYU South Campus Station. You can take the FrontRunner to Provo Station. The UVX bus leaves every 6 minutes from Provo Station heading toward the BYU South Campus Station and its about a 18 minute ride.
