Registration, Abstract Submission, and Payment

Please refer to the Guidelines for Presenters as you prepare your poster or talk.

There are three steps involved to register for the Air Quality: Science for Solutions conference. The sequence is not important.

  1. Pay the registration fee of $20.00 per person. ($40 after March 20, 2023)
  2. Register here.
  3. Submit your abstract for review if you would like to present at the conference. (All abstracts undergo a peer review by the conference committee.)

1. Payment:

This will take you to the Weber State University secure e-commerce site to pay for registrations. You can pay for multiple registrants at once. (Opens in a new browser tab.) All attendees (virtual or real) pay the same registration, please see the FAQ for details.

2. Registration

Opens in a new browser tab. Requires an accurate email to get a confirmation.

3. Abstract Submission

Opens in a new browser tab. Requires an accurate email to get a confirmation. Please submit abstracts as a pdf and limit them to 350 words.

All abstracts are reviewed by the committee before being accepted. While we try to honor requests for oral vs. poster formats, due to limited time only a small number of oral presentations are possible.
